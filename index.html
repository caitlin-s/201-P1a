Infrastructure of Wellington

Infrastructure plays a key role in the operation of our city, connecting everything to a wider network from a single person to a whole city. There are markers and indicators of this infrastructure dotted around the city and its suburbs in many different forms, each linking us with a multitude of vital networks. We sometimes don't appreciate the vast structures that are situated above and below us, and how important these structures are in maintaining and meeting our daily demands.
