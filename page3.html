Buried Cables

These buried cable markers can be found scattered around streets the streets of Wellington. They are indicators of the cables below us that form various systems which play a crucial part in our lives. It is, again, a reminder of the interconnecting structures and systems that we don't see. It shows us just how vast this framework of cables is and how important it is for connecting us.
